## Overview
Pallikari is a centaur with a rich history, having fought in Aramaina's army and maintained connections with key figures in the realm.

## Background
- **Race:** Centaur
- **Age:** Claims to be over 200 years old
- **Status:** Living legend among centaurs

## Historical Significance
- **Military Service:** Veteran of [[Dramatis-Personae#Aramaina Faelandaerl|Aramaina]]'s army
- **Turning Point:** Left military service after the burning of the Abbey of St. Richarius
- **Legacy:** Respected figure in centaur society

## Relationships
- **[[Dramatis-Personae#Corin Quickpath|Corin Quickpath]]:** Close friend and comrade-in-arms
- **[[Dramatis-Personae#Squire|Squire]]:** Knew each other from past adventures
- **Companions:** Has interacted with the main party on several occasions

## Quest Involvement
- **Current Mission:** Searching for Quickpath in the [[Places-of-Asterion#Forest of Stone|Forest of Stone]]
- **Belief:** Suspects Quickpath may have hidden the [[Artifacts-&-Treasures#Veil of Elphame|Veil of Elphame]] there
- **Motivation:** Wishes to protect the artifact and honor his friend's legacy
